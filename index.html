About

Freedom Farts is the politically-themed, satirical fart app for iOS that never was. It was originally submitted to the App Store in October, 2012 — just in time for the 2012 Presidential Election. (Wasn't that exciting!) Unfortunately, it was rejected by Apple because "too many fart apps".

However, Apple can't reject web apps. So, here it is — free for the web, for all to enjoy. Also, the original iOS app is now free and open-source, and available on GitHub.
